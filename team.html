The Team

Reserach & Creative Development

Research & Creative Development are responsible for gathering data and that data to update small businesses that are available. These interns also use the data to write articles on Generation Why's website. This department is led by Wenny Cheng.

Graphic Design

Graphic Design deals with the aesthetics of Generation Why. The team helps design the website and blog as well as make designs for a number of other things like Generation Why's logo. This department is led by Gabriela Pedreros.

Financial Planning & Budgeting

Financial Planning & Budgeting deals with the money in Generation Why. The team handles the budget and makes sure that each other deparment gets the money it needs. It also pitches and applies for grants and loans. The department is led by Stanley Zhao.

System Development

System Development handles the main website for Generation Why. The team helps perfect the search engine so that the user can find the small that he or she is looking for. The department is led by Avi Udash.

Marketing

Marketing is responsible for maintaining user attention and making Generation Why more popular. The Marketing team works on social media platforms like Instagram to get Generation Why more recognition. The department is led by Monica Min.

Human Resources

Human Resources is reponsible for ensuring that all members of Generation Why have equal access to resources that they need to efectively perform their work. The department is led by Sharon Li.

Business Administration & Legal

Business Administration & Legal's role is to manage Generation Why so that it is a business/corporation. The team also works closely with Human Resources if any issues or concerns arise. The department is led by Bryan Sanchez.
