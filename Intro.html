Generation Why

What It Is

Generation Why is a company that was started by Amherst University student Minju Kang. The goal of the company is to provide users with access to small businesses. The user can search for the name of a company with our search engine and find a number of businesses to work for.


The Team

Generation Why is divided up into 7 departments: Research & Creative Development, Graphic Design, Financial Planning & Budgeting, System Development, Human Resources, Marketing, and Business Administration & Legal.
